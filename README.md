# gitcli
批量下载gitlab中的多个仓库代码


gitcli -host https://gitlab.100bm.cn


```sh
> gitcli show groups

-------------------------------------------------------------------
17ebs      |  group    |ebs(Energy business systems) --- 能源业务系统，包括电子券业务，加油业务
-------------------------------------------------------------------
micro-plat |  group    |公共、基础项目
-------------------------------------------------------------------

> gitcli show projects
-------------------------------------------------------------------
17ebs      |ebs(Energy business systems) --- 能源业务系统，包括电子券业务，加油业务
-------------------------------------------------------------------
micro-plat |公共、基础项目
-------------------------------------------------------------------

> gitcli find project 17ebs
/17ebs/apiserver
/17ebs/flowserver
/17ebs/docs

> gitcli download 17ebs
```
